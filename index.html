Why can’t you see your account on the site?

Right now Twitter is cleaning up accounts, and many profiles are getting shadow bans.

The site looks for tweets in two places:

  1. By the @pharos_network tag in global search. My API may not see your posts because of a shadow ban.
  2. Inside the Pharos Network Official community . In most cases, tweets are still visible there even if your account is shadow banned.

Please post in the Pharos Network Official community and publish your content there. That gives your posts a much higher chance of being visible, because the site currently works only with public data.
